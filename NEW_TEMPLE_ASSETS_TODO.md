# 🎨 Temple Assets - Creation Guide

This document provides ChatGPT/AI prompts and instructions for creating assets for ALL temples (Hanuman, Ganesh, Shiva, Krishna, Durga).

## 🖼️ DEITY IMAGES

### 🙏 Hanuman Image (Hanuman.png)

**ChatGPT/DALL-E/Midjourney Prompt:**
```
Create a divine, powerful image of Lord Hanuman (Bajrangbali) in a devotional pose.
He should be shown with folded hands (namaste/pranaam) in devotion to Lord Ram.
Features to include:
- Orange/saffron colored body (sindoor)
- Muscular, powerful physique
- Long tail curled upward
- Wearing a golden crown or headpiece
- Mace (gada) resting nearby or held in one hand
- Rudraksha mala around neck
- Dhoti in saffron/yellow color
- Devotional, humble expression with eyes looking upward
- Subtle image of Lord Ram in his heart (optional)
- Background: Temple setting with warm orange/saffron glow, mountains
- Art style: Traditional Indian painting, vibrant saffron and gold tones
- Aspect ratio: Square (1:1)
- No text or watermarks
```

**Recommended Size:** 512x512px or 1024x1024px PNG

---

### 🐘 Ganesh Image (Ganesh.png)

**ChatGPT/DALL-E/Midjourney Prompt:**
```
Create a divine, auspicious image of Lord Ganesha (Ganapati) seated on a lotus throne.
He should be shown in a blessing pose (Abhaya mudra). Features to include:
- Elephant head with large ears and kind eyes
- One broken tusk (Ekadanta)
- Trunk curved to the left, holding a modak (sweet)
- Four arms holding:
  * Modak (sweet dumpling) in lower left
  * Axe (parashu) in upper right
  * Lotus in upper left
  * Lower right in blessing pose (abhaya mudra)
- Large belly representing the universe
- Mouse (Mushika) near his feet
- Crown decorated with jewels
- Red/pink or yellow dhoti
- Background: Auspicious yellow/golden glow, floral decorations
- Ornate jewelry and decorations
- Art style: Traditional Indian painting, warm gold and red tones
- Aspect ratio: Square (1:1)
- No text or watermarks
```

**Recommended Size:** 512x512px or 1024x1024px PNG

### 🔱 Shiva Image (Shiva.png)

**ChatGPT/DALL-E/Midjourney Prompt:**
```
Create a divine, serene image of Lord Shiva in meditation pose (padmasana). 
He should be seated on a tiger skin atop Mount Kailash with the Ganga river 
flowing from his matted locks (jata). Features to include:
- Third eye on forehead
- Crescent moon in his hair
- Cobra (Vasuki) around his neck
- Blue throat (Neelkanth)
- Trishul (trident) nearby
- Rudraksha beads
- Sacred ash (bhasma/vibhuti) on body
- Peaceful, meditative expression
- Background: Himalayan mountains, mystical blue/purple lighting
- Art style: Traditional Indian painting style, vibrant yet peaceful colors
- Aspect ratio: Square (1:1)
- No text or watermarks
```

**Recommended Size:** 512x512px or 1024x1024px PNG with transparent or solid color background

### 🦚 Krishna Image (Krishna.png)

**ChatGPT/DALL-E/Midjourney Prompt:**
```
Create a divine, enchanting image of Lord Krishna playing his flute (bansuri).
He should be standing in the Tribhanga pose (three-bend posture). Features:
- Blue/dark complexion (Shyam)
- Peacock feather in crown/hair (morpankh)
- Yellow/golden dhoti (pitambara)
- Peacock feather garland around neck
- Holding a flute to his lips
- Loving, playful smile
- Beautiful jewelry and ornaments
- Background: Vrindavan forest with flowering trees, cows grazing peacefully
- Radha silhouette can be suggested in background
- Art style: Traditional Indian painting, warm golden and blue tones
- Aspect ratio: Square (1:1)
- No text or watermarks
```

**Recommended Size:** 512x512px or 1024x1024px PNG

### 🦁 Durga Image (Durga.png)

**ChatGPT/DALL-E/Midjourney Prompt:**
```
Create a powerful, divine image of Goddess Durga (Mahishasuramardini).
She should be riding her lion (vahana) in a victorious pose. Features:
- Eight or ten arms holding divine weapons:
  * Trishul (trident)
  * Chakra (discus)
  * Sword
  * Bow and arrow
  * Lotus
  * Conch shell
  * Others
- Red/golden saree
- Crown/tiara (mukut)
- Third eye on forehead
- Fierce yet benevolent expression
- Red vermillion (sindoor) on forehead
- Background: Radiant red/golden aura, divine light
- Lion in powerful stance
- Art style: Traditional Indian painting, rich reds and golds
- Aspect ratio: Square (1:1)
- No text or watermarks
```

**Recommended Size:** 512x512px or 1024x1024px PNG

## 🎤 AI VOICE GENERATION OPTIONS

If you want to generate mantras using AI text-to-speech:

### Option 1: ElevenLabs
1. Use Hindi voice model
2. Paste Sanskrit mantra text
3. Adjust pace to slow/meditative
4. Download as MP3

### Option 2: Google Cloud TTS
1. Use Hindi-IN voice
2. SSML for proper pronunciation
3. Slower speaking rate (0.7-0.8)

### Mantra Texts to Convert:

**Shiva:**
```
ॐ नमः शिवाय। ॐ नमः शिवाय।
ॐ त्र्यम्बकं यजामहे सुगन्धिं पुष्टिवर्धनम्।
उर्वारुकमिव बन्धनान्मृत्योर्मुक्षीय माऽमृतात्॥
```

```
हरे कृष्ण हरे कृष्ण कृष्ण कृष्ण हरे हरे।
हरे राम हरे राम राम राम हरे हरे॥
```

**Durga:**

## 🔧 AFTER ADDING ASSETS

1. **Test the app:**
   ```bash
   ng serve
   ```

2. **Navigate to each temple** and verify:
   - Deity image loads correctly
   - Audio plays when aarti button is clicked
   - Animations work smoothly

3. **Optimize images if needed:**
   - Use TinyPNG for compression
   - Keep under 200KB per image

4. **Compress audio if needed:**
   - Use Audacity or online tools
   - Target: 128kbps, mono for smaller file size
